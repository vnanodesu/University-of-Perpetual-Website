About Us

Perpetual Help College of Pangasinan

Perpetual Help College of Pangasinan is a private academic institution in Malasiqui, Pangasinan. Founded in 1973, the school is part of the University of Perpetual Help System (UPHS). UPHS was established to specialize in health care programs; the college in Pangasinan fulfills this goal with its various health allied courses.
