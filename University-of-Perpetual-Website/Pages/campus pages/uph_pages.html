UPH-DR. JOSE G. TAMAYO MEDICAL UNIVERSITY

Dr. Jose G. Tamayo was a native founder of Malasiqui Pangasinan. He was born on September 10, 1920 at the Philippine General Hospital, Manila to Mariano Tamayo from Alcala, Pangasinan and maria De Guzman from Malasiqui, Pangasinan. He finished his studies as Doctor of Medicine at University of Sto. Tomas. His field of specialization is OB-Gynecology. He died on August 6, 2002 at the age of 82 years old.

Dra. Josefina laperal Tamayo was a native of Binan, Laguna. She was born on April 20, 1987. She finished her studies as Doctor of Medicine. She finished his studies as Doctor of Medicine at University of Sto. Tomas. Her field of specialization is Pediatrics. She died on February 18, 1920.

Dr. Jose (fondly called Dr. J) and Dr. Josefina (fondly called Dra. Nena) were marrried. They were blessed with twelve children: Antonio, Ma. Theresa, Marianito, Arcadio, Roberto, Manuel, Rafael, Marciana, Florenciana, Jose Mauro, Ma. Consorcia, and Joseph Victor.

The history of Perpetual Help System is classified into three stages. The Pre-foundation period (1944-1953); The Foundation (1965-1974); and the Satelliting Period (1973-present).
